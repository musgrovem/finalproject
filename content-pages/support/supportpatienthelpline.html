You are not alone. The AAMDSIF HelpLine has trained and compassionate patient educators ready to answer your questions and provide the support you need. To speak with a patient educator, please call (800) 747-2820 , option 1, or email help@aamds.org.

What do we do?

The AAMDSIF HelpLine has trained patient educators standing by to answer your questions about aplastic anemia, MDS or PNH. We can also provide emotional support, guidance and referrals to national and local resources.

Who do we help?

We can help anyone interested in or affected by a bone marrow failure disease. This includes:

  • People living with aplastic anemia, MDS, PNH or related bone marrow failure diseases
  • Caregivers and friends of someone living with a bone marrow failure disease
  • Anyone who simply wants to learn more about these diseases

How can we help you?

The AAMDS HelpLine provides telephone support, answers emails and responds to online posts.

Some of the topics our patient educators respond to most often include:

  • Understanding bone marrow failure diseases
  • Available treatment options
  • Finding an expert in your disease
  • Complementary and alternative therapies
  • Support groups and AAMDSIF Communities of Hope
  • Emotional support
  • Getting connected with Peer Support Network volulnteer
  • Educational Conferences and courses
  • Requests for FREE print materials
  • Referrals to resources that may provide financial assistance
